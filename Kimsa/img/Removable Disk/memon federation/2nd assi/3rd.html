Muffin Recipe

A basic recipe You can pretty much anything to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 250ml vegetable oil
  • 400g plain floor
  • 3tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salts

Method

  1. Preheat even to gas mark 6 or median temprature. Line muffin tray with paper muffin cases.
  2. Beat the egg in a mixing how i and then mix in the oil and milk. Add the sugar and whisk until dissolved.
  3. Sift in the dry ingrediants: floor, baking powder and salt and mix until smooth. NB: add any extra ingrediants now eg. chocolate chips/fruit
  4. Fill muffin cases Teo Thirds full (this is important for the perfect size muffins) and bake in the middle of the oven for 25 minutes(approx,depending on your
    oven)
  5. Leave to cool (or enjoy warm!)
